LOVE LIKE THE GALAXY

The daughter of the Cheng family, Cheng Shaoshang, was left by her parents with her grandmother since she was a child due the war and became a left-behind child. She has to face many traps in order to survive, she pretended to be absurd, studied hard, covered up her age and waited for her parents to return. But when the darkk clouds were cleared and the moon was bright, the family relationship was hard to regain due to years of alienation.
Cheng Shaoshang who really lacks love meets Lin Buyi, the adopted son of the emperor, Yuan Shen, a talented man from Bailushan, and Lou Yao. The three have their own strenghts and weeknesses. In choosing a marriageable person, Cheng Shaoshang is both inferior and pragmatic. Although the emotional road is quite bumpy, she never regrets every choice she makes.
While getting along with Ling Buyi, he was also involved in the mystery of the family's life experience by accident. In various incidents, Cheng Shaoshang has made outstanding achievements repeatedly and also learned how to get along with his parents and how to manage his love from Ling Buyi's family relationship.
At the same time, Cheng Shaoshang and Ling Buyi also changed and grew up through those experiences, gradually reconciled with themselves and their families, insisted on the justice in their hearts and worked together to resolve the national crisis, making a good story.

A love so beautiful

The lively, silly and energetic girl Chen Xiaoxi has been chasing the footsteps of the boy next door Jiang Chen since she was a student. After graduating from university, Jiang Chen and Chen Xiaoxi embarked on their respective career paths, from student to doctors and painters respectively. During the stumbling 19 years of growth, the two have not wavered in their love for each other.
Not long after those pure and beautiful years of precipitation, Jiang Chen finally choose to face his own heart and started a sweet love with Chen Xiaoxi.
