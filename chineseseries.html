Leo Wu is a young Chinese actor. He is 1.82 m (6ft 0 in) and born on 26 December 1999 in Shanghai (China). He is also known by his Chinese name Wu Lei. Other name's: Lei Lei, China's little brother. He was active since 2002 till present. His first appearance was in a commercials at the age of 3. In 2011 he won th Best Young Actor at the 28th Flying Apsaras Awards for his role in Mo's Mischief.
